1. Condition - Cancellation Policy

In case of booking cancellation, the refunds would be made as explained below:

1.)Cancellation before 7 days: 80% Refund.
2.)Cancellation between 7 days-1 day: 70% Refund.
3.)Cancellation within 24 hours: No Refund.

2. Condition - Child Policy

1.)Child below age of 10 yrs will be complimentary. And above age of 10 child will be charged as adult.

3. Condition - Lodge Policy

1.)Hotel may charge supplement charges on Christmas / New Year's eve or other festive periods. Any such supplements need to be paid directly at the hotel.
2.)Early check-in will be provided on the basis of availability or on a prior notice.
3.)Pick up and drop at additional cost from Nearest airport or railway station on prior intimation can be provided.
